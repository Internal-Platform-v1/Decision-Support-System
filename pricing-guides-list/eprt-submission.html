Key Reminders
Use Customer Discount Loss or Incorrect Pricing in most cases.
Use AWB or PRO tracking numbers only. Do not enter invoice numbers.
Attach screenshots, PDFs, XLS files, or pricing proof when needed.
Do not submit full invoice spreadsheet audits through iSell.
