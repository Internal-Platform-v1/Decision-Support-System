ePRT Submission Guide

Use this guide to submit an ePRT ticket correctly in iSell, complete the required case details, add supporting documents, and avoid delays caused by missing or incorrect information.

Submission Steps
Follow in order
1

Open iSell

Go to the iSell portal to begin creating the ePRT ticket.

Open iSell
2

Navigate to My Cases

Click My Cases from the top dashboard in iSell.

3

Create a New Case

Click New Case to start the request.

4

Select Pricing Option

Choose Pricing. Hovering over the option will show the issues it applies to.

5

Choose the Correct Issue Type

Select the applicable radio button, then click Next.

Important
Do not select Non-Sales Pricing Request unless you are a non-sales user.

In most cases, select Customer Discount Loss or Incorrect Pricing.

6

Confirm Employee ID

Your employee ID should appear automatically. Confirm it, then click Next.

7

Enter Customer EAN

Enter the Customer EAN related to the issue, then click Next.

8

Complete the Case Form

Fill out the form using the information from the ePRT Ticket Support Form.

Reminder
All fields marked with * are mandatory. Select the most accurate option available.
9

Add the Issue Description

Provide clear details about the pricing issue, expected discount, missing exception, or expected surcharge handling.

Example
Customer AWBs for Express SO shipments are not receiving the expected volume discount.
  • Expected ED percentage
  • Expected surcharge exception
  • Other agreement details that support the request
Do Not
Do not request full invoice spreadsheet audits through iSell. Provide only a few AWB or PRO examples.
10

Submit the Case

After submission, choose View Case. You can then upload files and finish the process.

11

Add Additional Case Details

Complete the added case details so the ePRT Team can review the request properly.

  • Proposal Number — valid proposal number of the agreement
  • Tracking Numbers — AWBs or PRO numbers, maximum of 5
  • Issue Description — add more detail if needed
  • Files — upload screenshots, PDFs, XLS files, or other support
Important
Do not enter invoice numbers. Use AWB or PRO tracking numbers only.
12

ePRT Case Review

The GPRT team will review the case and may request additional details in the case comments.

Once all details are provided, the case will be worked or escalated if needed.

13

Request Updates

To request updates, send an email from OSV and include the Salesforce Case Number.

pricing_ePRT@corp.ds.fedex.com
14

Add a Customer Profile Note

Add a Customer Profile note using the format below.

DATE – VS-PRIC-###### – EPRT TCKT # XXXXX – REASON
09/22/22 – VS-PRIC-14599820 – EPRT TCKT #47103 – RVW NOST WAIVED PER AGREEMENT
15

Pend the Salesforce Case

Pend the Salesforce case and include the ePRT Ticket Number in the Internal Comments.

Completed
Submit the ePRT request with complete and accurate details, then document the ticket number properly so the case can be tracked without delays.
