Key Reminders
Change the freight charge rate line to FLAT and apply the Volume Quote amount.
Remove rate keywords such as FSCL, DEWT, RAZ, LEDI, PRAU, and BRAP.
Capacity load keywords do not need to be removed.
Click ACCEPT ALL after completing the correction.
