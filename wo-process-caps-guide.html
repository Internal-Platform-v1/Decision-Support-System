CAPS Write-Off Procedure

Process a write‑off in CAPS from start to finish.

Follow these steps to select the correct Business Unit, navigate the write‑off screens, enter PROs and reason codes, add the required FBC comment, respond to the sales rep, and close the case.

Key Reminders
Always select the correct Business Unit (ARFW or FXFC) based on the invoice debtor BU.
Ensure the Reason Code matches the valid justification for the write-off.
Use the exact FBC comment template and Sales Rep response template.
Email imagewriteoffs@fedexfreight.com — no separate form is required.
Update Case Count and WO AMT when closing the case.
