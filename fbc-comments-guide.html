Reminder

Important FBC comment reminder

  1. Always enter FBC Comment (F9) even if no corrections or updates are made on the PROs.
  2. Always enter an FBC/FBI comment on the PRO every time you open the PRO in FBI/FBC and/or if you provided different information or resolution on the customer dispute.

For the Case # — Enter the case number of the actual case you are working. Example: if a clone case is assigned to you, in your FBC comment, enter the case number of the actual clone case you are working.
NOTE: This is just a template that serves as your guide. You can always make a revision on it to suit the resolution on the case.
