Reminder

Important FBC comment reminder

  1. Always enter FBC Comment (F9) even if no corrections or updates are made on the PROs.
  2. Always enter an FBC/FBI comment on the PRO every time you open the PRO in FBI/FBC and/or if you provided different information or resolution on the customer dispute.
