Decision Guide Access

Sign in securely using a one-time email link. This helps protect access without requiring a password.

  • Enter your approved work email to receive a one-time sign-in link
  • Your email is verified as part of the sign-in process
  • Open the link from your inbox to complete secure access

Sign in with email link

Enter your approved email address and a secure sign-in link will be sent to your inbox.

Use the same browser on the same device for the smoothest sign-in experience.
